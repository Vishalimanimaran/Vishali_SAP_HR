## Overview
Employee lifecycle management tracks employees from hire to exit.

## Key Lifecycle Events
- Hire
- Rehire
- Promotion
- Transfer
- Job Change
- Termination

## Hire Process
- Candidate data mapped to employee profile
- Workflow approval
- System validations applied

## Job Change / Promotion
- Update job information
- Business rules triggered
- Manager & HR approvals

## Termination
- End of employment
- Data retention and access control

## Outcome
Ensures accurate employee data and compliance across HR operations
