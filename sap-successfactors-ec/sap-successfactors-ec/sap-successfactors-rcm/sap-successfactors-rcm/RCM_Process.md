## RCM Process Flow
1. Job Requisition Creation
2. Job Posting
3. Candidate Application Collection
4. Resume Screening
5. Interview Scheduling
6. Interview Feedback
7. Offer Creation
8. Offer Approval
9. Onboarding Initiation

## Recruiter Responsibilities
- Post jobs to relevant portals
- Screen resumes and shortlist candidates
- Schedule interviews with hiring managers
- Collect and process interview feedback
- Manage offer letters and approvals

## Hiring Manager Responsibilities
- Approve job requisitions
- Participate in interviews
- Provide timely feedback
- Approve offers
