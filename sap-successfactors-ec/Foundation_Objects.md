# Foundation Objects in SAP SuccessFactors EC

## What are Foundation Objects?
Foundation Objects store organizational structure data used across Employee Central.

## Common Foundation Objects
- Legal Entity
- Business Unit
- Division
- Department
- Location
- Job Classification
- Position

## Why They Are Important
- Used in Job Information
- Support reporting & analytics
- Enable position management

## Configuration Highlights
- MDF based objects
- Effective dated
- Controlled using Role-Based Permissions (RBP)
